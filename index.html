About Us

Fall in love with flowers all over again

Why Choose us?

Petal Shower Floral Arrangements was established in 2021 by Ms. Maria Robinson when she realized that she was unable to get beautifully arranged bouquets from locally grown flower blossoms.

Having been exposed to horticulture at a young age and understanding some of the challenges farmers can face she decided to create her business and support local horticulturists.

All while reintroducing the love of ‘real’ flowers again to the public and showing how they can be used in a variety of arrangements for various events.
